Benefits of RSA

  • Towing Service

  • Battery Jump Service

  • Emergency Gas Service

  • Flat Tire Assistance

  • Key Lockout Service

Towing Service

In the event the vehicle is unable to safely proceed under it's own power, the RSA Provider will arrange to have the vehicle transported to nearest qualified repair service facility and will pay up to a maximum of $200 per occurence for the transportation expenses. In order to be transported, the vehicle must be accesible from an incorporated paved road.

Contact RSA

Battery Jump Service

In the event the vehcile will not crank due to a weak or "run-down" battery, the RSA Provider will arrange for a service provider to boost or jump-start the battery and will pay up to a maximum of $150 per occurrence for the battery jump service.

Contact RSA

Emergency Gas Service

In the event the vehicle runs out of gas, the RSA Provider will arrange for a service provider to deliver an emergency supply of gas for the vehicle and will pay up to a maximum of $150 per occurence for the gas delivery service, excluding the cost of the gas. You are responsible for the cost of the emergency supply of gas at the time of delivery.

Contact RSA

Flat Tire Assistance

In the event of a flat tire on the vehicle, the RSA Provider will arrange for a service provider to mount an inflated spare tire provided by you and will pay up to a maximum of $150 per occurence for the flat tire service call. If an inflated spare tire is not available, then the RSA Provider will arrange to have the vehicle transported to the nearest tire center.

Contact RSA

Key Lockout Service

In the event the keys for the vehicle are lost, broken, or accidentally locked in the vehicle, or the vehicle has a frozen lock, the RSA Provider will arrange for a service provider to unlock the vehicle and will pay up to a maximum of $150 per occurrence for the locksmith service, excluding the cost of replacement keys. You are responsible for the cost of any replacement keys at the time of service.

Contact RSA

  • About RSA

  • What is the Roadside Assistance (RSA) Program?

    The Royal Enfield Roadside Assistance Program is a 24x7 emergency support provided in the event of a breakdown of the user's motorcycle, anywhere on state highways or motor-able roads within the United States, Canada, and at user's home and/or place of work. Services are not available in areas where state providers are exclusively utilized or if the vehicle is more than 100 feet from an incorporated or paved road. The membership begins on the new 650 Twin date of purchase and contues for three years.

  • Who provides Roadside Assistance services?

    Roadside Assistance is available through our partner SafeRide Motor Club 1-888-777-5907

  • How do I contact the service provider, and when are they available?

    24 hours, 7 days a week at 1-888-777-5907

  • Can I transfer or cancel my RSA membership?

    No, Royal Enfield North America RSA memberships ony apply to new 650 Twins and are not alterable.

  • What are the benefits of RSA?

    - Towing service
    - Flat tire change service
    - Emergency gas delivery service
    - Battery jump service
    - Key lockout service

  • How long will my membership last?

    Royal Enfield will cover RSA for three years after purchase date of new 650 Twin motorcycle.
