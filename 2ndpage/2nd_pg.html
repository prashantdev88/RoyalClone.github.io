Retro Racing At Its Purest

A racing format that harks back to a time when the only ingredients a rider depended on to succeed was his own ability, grit, and will. Simple, very capable and reliable machines that offered no electronic rider aids, making the slightest mistake unforgiving on the race track. It was a time when winning came second to the challenge, pure adrenaline rush and fun of competing. This is what motorcycle racing began as, this is what the GT cup is bringing back!
